# Content Hooks — [Brand Name]

---

> Content should meet buyers where they are — not where you want them to be.
> Each stage of the Buyer Belief Ladder requires a different hook strategy.
> Hooks that pitch too far ahead of the buyer's current belief get ignored or create distrust.

---

## Hooks by Belief Stage

---

### Stage 1 — Unaware
**Target:** Buyers who don't yet recognize they have a problem worth solving. They're scrolling past, not searching.

**Goal:** Create pattern interruption. Name a situation they recognize even if they don't see it as a problem yet.

**Hook Ideas:**
- [e.g. "Most [ICP] don't realize [surprising fact about their situation]"]
- [e.g. "If you've ever [common experience], this is why it keeps happening"]
- [e.g. "Nobody talks about [uncomfortable truth in their world]"]

---

### Stage 2 — Problem Recognition
**Target:** Buyers who know something's wrong but haven't named it or found a cause. They're frustrated and searching.

**Goal:** Validate the pain and name it precisely. Make them feel seen.

**Hook Ideas:**
- [e.g. "You're not imagining it — [problem] is real and here's why it matters"]
- [e.g. "The hidden cost of ignoring [problem] (most people find out too late)"]
- [e.g. "Why [common workaround] doesn't actually fix the problem"]

---

### Stage 3 — Cause Awareness
**Target:** Buyers who understand the problem but don't know why their previous attempts failed. They're skeptical but still looking.

**Goal:** Reframe the problem so your solution makes logical sense. Teach before you sell.

**Hook Ideas:**
- [e.g. "The real reason [common solution] doesn't work for [ICP]"]
- [e.g. "3 things [ICP] keep doing that make [problem] worse — and what to do instead"]
- [e.g. "I tried everything too. Here's what I missed."]

---

### Stage 4 — Solution Awareness
**Target:** Buyers who are open to a solution and evaluating whether it could work for them. They're comparing and researching.

**Goal:** Show the mechanism working for someone like them. Proof over promise.

**Hook Ideas:**
- [e.g. "How [client profile] went from [before] to [after] in [timeframe] using [approach]"]
- [e.g. "Here's the exact process we used to get [specific result] for [type of person]"]
- [e.g. "[Before/after story] — and the one thing that actually made the difference"]

---

### Stage 5 — Provider Decision
**Target:** Buyers who are ready to act and choosing between options. They're evaluating trust and fit.

**Goal:** Remove risk, establish credibility, and make the decision easy. Differentiate from alternatives.

**Hook Ideas:**
- [e.g. "Why [ICP] choose [Brand Name] over [common alternative]"]
- [e.g. "What to ask before you [hire / buy / sign up for] any [category]"]
- [e.g. "[Guarantee / risk-removal statement] — here's what we promise and what happens if we miss"]
