## Proof Requirements by Belief Stage

Different stages need different proof. Using the wrong type at the wrong stage wastes proof assets and loses buyers.

| Stage | Buyer State | Proof Type Needed | Example |
|---|---|---|---|
| Stage 1 — Situation Awareness | Doesn't know they have a problem worth solving | Social proof of problem existence — others like them are experiencing it | [e.g. "You're not alone: 73% of [ICP] struggle with X"] |
| Stage 2 — Problem Recognition | Knows there's a problem, doesn't know the cause | Educational validation — shows their experience is real and has a root cause | [e.g. Blog post: "Why X keeps happening even when you try Y"] |
| Stage 3 — Cause Awareness | Understands the cause, doubts solutions work | Case study proof — someone like them got results with this approach | [e.g. "How [client profile] went from X to Y in Z time using this method"] |
| Stage 4 — Solution Awareness | Knows solutions work, evaluating options | Mechanism proof — shows why this specific method works | [e.g. Breakdown of the process, behind-the-scenes, before/after with explanation] |
| Stage 5 — Provider Decision | Ready to buy, comparing providers | Provider trust proof — credibility, safety, fit | [e.g. Testimonials about experience working with [Brand Name], guarantees, credentials, response time] |
